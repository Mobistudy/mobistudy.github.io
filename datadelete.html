Delete your account and/or data:

You can delete your account using the app, by opening the profile section and clicking on "Delete Account". This will delete all the data permanently for all studies no the Mobistudy server, but data that has been downloaded by researchers for analysis will not be deleted (and often cannot be deleted due to research archival laws). If you cannot use the app to delete your account, if you want to delete only some speficic information, if you want to have some of your data corrected, or for any other question please contact the Mobistudy team at mobistudy@mau.se. Read the Privacy Policy for further information about how your data is handled and your rights.

Mobistudy

An open platform for mobile health research.

Mobistudy is an open mobile-health platform for clinical research. The purpose of the platform is to create a way for researchers to conduct health research without the the need for developing new apps. The platform has an emphasis on regulatory compliance, patient consent and transparency.

For clinical researchers:

  • Define your study with an online form
  • Recruit patients online
  • Monitor status and progession
  • Download study data for analysis

For citizens:

  • Be involved in interesting research
  • Easy to understand and revokable consent
  • Donate your data for research
  • Perform measurement tasks, be informed abuot your health

Features

  • Researchers can setup new studies without the need of a developer
  • Several data collection tasks:
    • Forms and questionnaires
    • Data collection from common apps and wearables through HealthKit and Google Fit
    • Six-minute walk tetst
    • Queen's College Step Test
  • Works on iPhones and Android phones
  • User friendly
  • Transparent and trustful
